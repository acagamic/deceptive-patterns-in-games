## How it works

Content sold at a premium — a season bundle, a founder's pack, paid DLC — is later released separately at a lower price, or made free, leaving early full-price buyers worse off for having bought in good faith.

## Why it can be harmful

It penalises trust and loyalty: the players most willing to support a game overpay relative to those who simply waited. Repeated, it erodes confidence that any purchase reflects fair value and trains players to distrust launch-window pricing.
