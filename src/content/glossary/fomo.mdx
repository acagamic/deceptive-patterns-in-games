---
term: "FOMO"
aliases: ["fear of missing out", "fading opportunities"]
short: "Manufactured fear of missing out, used to shortcut deliberation through scarcity and urgency."
seeAlso: ["fomo-limited-time-offers", "retention-mechanics"]
references: ["petrovskaya-zendle-2022", "sousa-2023"]
---

Limited-time banners, expiring bundles, and countdowns manufacture urgency that pressures impulsive action. As a decision-point tactic it is especially risky for minors and when offers are triggered by behavioural data at moments of heightened susceptibility.
