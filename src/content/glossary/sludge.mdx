---
term: "Sludge"
aliases: ["administrative friction"]
short: "Excessive friction deliberately placed in the user's protective path — the opposite of a helpful nudge."
seeAlso: ["friction", "obstructed-exit-sludge", "coercion"]
references: ["newall-2025", "kelly-2024"]
---

Sludge is friction engineered to discourage choices that protect the user — cancelling, refunding, opting out, or closing an account. Where a nudge eases a beneficial action, sludge obstructs one. It is the mechanism behind 'roach motel' designs that are easy to enter and hard to leave.
