---
term: "Gamblification"
aliases: ["gamblification of games"]
short: "The migration of gambling structures and aesthetics into mainstream digital games."
seeAlso: ["loot-box", "variable-ratio-reinforcement"]
references: ["brock-2021"]
---

Gamblification describes how chance-based reward, wagering aesthetics, and monetised risk have spread from gambling into ordinary games — blurring the boundary that regulation has historically relied on and raising public-health concerns.
