Opting into a magic show licenses deception within that frame — but consent is undermined when the mechanism is covert, the user is not competent to consent (e.g., children), the 'consent' was itself produced by a dark pattern, or exit is blocked. Consent obtained under aggressive dark patterns can be legally voidable, and awareness does not equal resistance.
